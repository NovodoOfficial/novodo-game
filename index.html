Commands

/shop • Open the shop

/ah • Open the auction house

/f create [faction name] • Create faction

/f roster add [player] [coleader,mod,member,recruit] • Give player permission to join your faction

/f invite [player] • Invite player to faction

/f join [faction name] • Join faction

Notice:
Use Shift + Backtick to record last 30s of gameplay in case of combat log or server issue.
Backtick is the key under Escape
